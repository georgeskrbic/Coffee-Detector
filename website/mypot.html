Fully equipped for a wireless world.

MyPot is designed to fit effortlessly into our increasingly wireless world. Just about anything you do with a coffee pot can now be done over the air, thanks to MyPot software that takes full advantage of the latest Wi-Fi wireless technology. So no matter where you are, you can connect to MyPot, brew a cup, check coffee analytics, and more - without being tied down.
